about

Hi, I'm Goose.
Welcome to Doodle Goose! I'm a practising illustrator and creative freelancer. I've been tweaking and ...beaking my drawings and designs since the day my egg first hatched.

Here, you'll find examples of my work style and personality - with some attempts to quack you up, too.

So without feather ado, owl let you get to it!

Freehand Illustration

All of my illustrations come straight from my heart and through my hand. Not to be blunt but... I always have a pencil sharpener in my backpack. Whatever your design, it'll be hand-drawn with love.

If you think up something I can't draw... I'll eat my own feathers.

Intricate Design

Over here in the cosy offices of Doodle Goose we have a full collection of high-end facilites and equipment. Technically, this allows me to help you express your artistic potential to any level of intricacy.

I will encompass your creative vision with a compass and precision.

Pixel Perfection

In the case of digital work, all of my designs are easily transferable to accommodate any media. If you have specific requirements I will be able to personally ensure optimisation and a completely tailored experience.

Doodle Goose rules! No one else can ...measure up!
